# Du'as and Prayers for Janazah

*A collection of essential prayers and supplications for the time of loss*

---

## إِنَّا لِلَّهِ وَإِنَّا إِلَيْهِ رَاجِعُونَ

**Inna lillahi wa inna ilayhi raji'un**

*Indeed, to Allah we belong and to Him we shall return.*

— Quran 2:156

This is the first words we say upon hearing of a loss. It reminds us of our return to our Creator and brings peace to the heart.

---

## Du'a When Visiting the Bereaved

> اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لَهُ وَارْحَمْهُ وَعَافِهِ وَاعْفُ عَنْهُ

**Allahumma-ghfir lahu warhamhu wa 'afihi wa'fu 'anhu**

*O Allah, forgive them, have mercy on them, give them wellbeing and pardon them.*

---

## Du'a During Ghusl (Ritual Washing)

Begin with:

> بِسْمِ اللَّهِ

**Bismillah** — *In the name of Allah*

Throughout, maintain silence or recite dhikr quietly. The ghusl is an act of worship.

---

## Salat al-Janazah (Funeral Prayer)

The funeral prayer consists of four takbirs (saying "Allahu Akbar"):

### After the First Takbir
Recite Surah Al-Fatihah (silently)

### After the Second Takbir
Send blessings upon the Prophet ﷺ:

> اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَعَلَى آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ كَمَا صَلَّيْتَ عَلَى إِبْرَاهِيمَ وَعَلَى آلِ إِبْرَاهِيمَ إِنَّكَ حَمِيدٌ مَجِيدٌ

### After the Third Takbir
Make du'a for the deceased:

> اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لَهُ وَارْحَمْهُ، وَعَافِهِ، وَاعْفُ عَنْهُ، وَأَكْرِمْ نُزُلَهُ، وَوَسِّعْ مُدْخَلَهُ، وَاغْسِلْهُ بِالْمَاءِ وَالثَّلْجِ وَالْبَرَدِ، وَنَقِّهِ مِنَ الْخَطَايَا كَمَا يُنَقَّى الثَّوْبُ الأَبْيَضُ مِنَ الدَّنَسِ، وَأَبْدِلْهُ دَارًا خَيْرًا مِنْ دَارِهِ، وَأَهْلاً خَيْرًا مِنْ أَهْلِهِ، وَزَوْجًا خَيْرًا مِنْ زَوْجِهِ، وَأَدْخِلْهُ الْجَنَّةَ، وَأَعِذْهُ مِنْ عَذَابِ الْقَبْرِ وَعَذَابِ النَّارِ

**Translation**: *O Allah, forgive them and have mercy on them. Give them wellbeing and pardon them. Honor their reception and broaden their entrance. Wash them with water, snow, and hail. Purify them from sins as a white garment is purified from dirt. Exchange their home for a better home, their family for a better family, and their spouse for a better spouse. Admit them to Paradise and protect them from the punishment of the grave and the Fire.*

### After the Fourth Takbir
A brief du'a, then salam to the right.

---

## Du'a for a Child Who Has Passed

> اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْهُ فَرَطًا وَذُخْرًا لِوَالِدَيْهِ، وَشَفِيعًا مُجَابًا

**Allahumma-j'alhu faratan wa dhukhran li-walidayhi, wa shafi'an mujaban**

*O Allah, make them a preceding reward and a stored treasure for their parents, and an accepted intercessor.*

---

## Du'a at the Graveside

When lowering the deceased:

> بِسْمِ اللَّهِ وَعَلَى مِلَّةِ رَسُولِ اللَّهِ

**Bismillahi wa 'ala millati Rasulillah**

*In the name of Allah and upon the way of the Messenger of Allah.*

---

## Du'a After Burial

The Prophet ﷺ would stand at the grave after burial and say:

> اسْتَغْفِرُوا لِأَخِيكُمْ، وَسَلُوا لَهُ التَّثْبِيتَ، فَإِنَّهُ الآنَ يُسْأَلُ

*Seek forgiveness for your brother/sister, and ask for their steadfastness, for they are now being questioned.*

---

## Words of Comfort for the Bereaved

> إِنَّ لِلَّهِ مَا أَخَذَ وَلَهُ مَا أَعْطَى وَكُلُّ شَيْءٍ عِنْدَهُ بِأَجَلٍ مُسَمًّى فَلْتَصْبِرْ وَلْتَحْتَسِبْ

*Indeed, to Allah belongs what He has taken, and to Him belongs what He has given. Everything has an appointed term with Him. So be patient and seek reward.*

---

## Dhikr for the Days of Grief

During the days of mourning, it brings comfort to recite:

- **Surah Yasin** — The heart of the Quran
- **Surah Al-Mulk** — Protection in the grave
- **Istighfar** — Seeking forgiveness
- **Salawat** — Sending blessings on the Prophet ﷺ

---

*May Allah grant patience to those who mourn and mercy to those who have departed.*

*Ameen.*
