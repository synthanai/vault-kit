# MANIFESTO: Vault-Kit Privacy Architecture (C4)

> **One-line:** AI systems that can prove what they DON'T know. Privacy by architecture, not by policy.

---

Vault-Kit implements a 3-layer privacy protocol: access control (who can see), audit trail (who DID see), and zero-knowledge dissent (disagree without revealing identity). The Janazah Protocol provides secure data sunset. The Wisdom Gate provides privacy-preserving access to sensitive cultural knowledge.

The contribution: a privacy architecture for AI deliberation systems that enables structured disagreement while protecting participant identity.
