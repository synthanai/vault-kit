# MANIFESTO: ZK-Dissent Protocol (G4)

> **One-line:** Disagree without revealing your identity. Zero-knowledge proofs for organizational dissent.

ZK-Dissent enables anonymous, verifiable disagreement in organizational decision-making. Participants can prove they qualify to dissent (role, seniority, domain expertise) without revealing WHO they are. Threshold revelation: dissent becomes visible to the group only when N independent dissenters agree, preventing lone-wolf suppression.

The contribution: applying zero-knowledge cryptographic constructions to organizational governance, enabling safe disagreement in power-asymmetric environments.
